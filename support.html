About Us

Moving to a new home is a significant event that is often stressful, and MoverZfax would like to help you, the customer, make a sound decision on how to select your mover. However, with so many choices out there, how to make that right choice? More tools are available at your fingertips but research can be dreadful. Tons of hours on the net to get information that might not be relevant on how to select that reputable mover. So what to do? At moverzfax, our team will do everything in their power to make your search easy. We will go through the numerous sites, complaints portals, recommendation reviews so you can have a peace of mind when your get the full report. Do not take yourself at the last minute to request that search. Many sites always recommend at least 6 to 8 weeks before your move. Make sure you do follow that time frame. Our team will go into details, will dig as much as possible about that specific company. We will go to the core to make sure you get the necessary information you need to make that right selection. Why do we do that? To eliminate the rogue movers from the market and allow only reputable moving company to do business in an honest way.

There are around 32% of the 35000 that are scammers. 33% chances of getting scammed. However, Moverzfax is here to ELIMINATE these rogue movers.

You, our customer, will no longer be a victim. You, our customer, will understand that cheap will ALWAYS be expensive. Give yourself that time and invest in your belongings that might possibly, if the research is not properly done, be taken in hostage by the wrong mover. We provide you with the tools; you will be the one making that final decision. MoverzFax is arming you with the information you need to protect yourself against one of the most stressful situations that can plague a household move: being taken advantage of by a dishonest or rogue mover. Make your decision the right ONE.

A Little Background

Tons of website out there provide online recommendations about moving companies. However, they are all dispersed which can make the research dreadful for many, hence, ending up choosing the wrong mover. Moverzfax is regrouping all websites under ONE roof, including but not limited to: Federal and state search, recommendation portals, moving association search, state registrations, federal registrations, legal status of company, and many more. A moving company can ONLY hide for a while, but not for long, especially with Moverzfax by your side. So give it a try, you'll be glad you did.

Where to Begin

If you are planning to move, research about moving company is a must and tons of information is available on the web. Protectyourmove.org is a great place to find information about rogue moving company as well as information about regulations for moving company, you can also look at movingscam.com or movingsham.com where companies are available. Also the Better Business Bureau of your local state can provide information about the moving company. As I said, tons of information is available online for you to search. With Moverzfax, we will go deeper into the search and grade (in %) the company based on many factors that will allow you to select them based on our research.
